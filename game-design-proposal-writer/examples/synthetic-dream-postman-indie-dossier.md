## Creative Thesis

This game is about delivering messages through a city whose rules can be rewritten, then living with the emotional and spatial consequences of each rewrite. The core fantasy is not being a postal worker in a dream setting; it is becoming the person who changes the city just enough to let a message arrive.

The design refuses to become a content-heavy open-city RPG. It should stay compact, modular, readable, and consequence-driven.

## Design Pillars

| pillar | design meaning | cut rule |
| --- | --- | --- |
| rules are visible magic | every card must change a route or social rule players can observe | cut cards with only stat effects |
| delivery creates consequence | each successful delivery changes someone or somewhere | cut quests with no aftermath |
| compact dream city | reuse districts through rule variation | cut large empty traversal |
